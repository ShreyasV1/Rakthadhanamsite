Contribute



Just like how little drops of water make a mighty ocean, every drop of blood donated can make a difference in the life of an individual.

If you'd like to contribute, you can choose to be a part of any of the following initiatives for you to show society how much you care.

1. We conduct blood donation camps in association with our partners every month.

2. We make it a point to conduct awareness camps in schools and colleges once in two weeks to instill the importance of blood and the advantages of blood donation.

Last but not the least any forms of monetary contributions is always welcome. Please do send your cheques in the name of Rakthadhanam Foundation, Chennai and your contributions are exempted from tax under the 80/c rule.
