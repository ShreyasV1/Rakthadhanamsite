About Us

How we're making a difference

We're a Non-profit organization started in 2017, with the aim of contributing to society. Every year, there is a demand of 5 crore blood units in India compared to the availability of only a meagre 2.5 crore blood units. To bridge this gap, we conduct various blood donation and awareness camps.

Get Involved!

Do you want to give back to society? it's never too late! If you'd like to volunteer or participate in any of our initiatives, check out the events planned out in the Upcoming Initiatives section and do reach out to us if you've got any queries.

Thank you!

Our heart felt thanks to you for visiting our site and contributing in any possible way by participating in our programmes or by spreading word of mouth.
